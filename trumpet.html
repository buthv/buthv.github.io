The Trumpet

The project is to design a unique shopping websites where you can find exclusive and innovative luxury fashion items, focusing on editorial pages. Using typography you need to find a way to show a sophisticated lifestyle journey through the worlds of fashion, high-tech and gadgets.
My Role : UI designer, Digital typography

The aim of this project is to design a luxury shopping websites called The Trumpet using the right choice of typography for digital devices. First, was to design The Trumpet logo, only with typography and then create the brand identity for the appearance of the website design layouts. The website consisted of homepage, editorial pages, news, fashion stories, Interviews, Lifetyle, products gallery, product details and product cart.
