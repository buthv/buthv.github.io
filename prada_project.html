My Role : Digital art direction

My approach on this project was to deeper understand the brand Prada. One way is to know about Fondazione Prada, which is either an art gallery, a public museum or a private foundation founded by Miuccia Prada and Patrizio Bertelli since 1995.

The intention for the architecture was to make old and new work seamlessly, where people wont be able to tell at any moment, whether they are in an old building or a new one. This is the concept behind the brand Prada.

The Digital Concept

For my project I decided to use the concept of new and old for the brand website design. On the homepage, there would be new products section and the timeless, iconic products.

At the timeless, iconic products section, customers could find collections and products that the brand could still have available for customers but are hidden on the website.

Customers could purchase products from past collections.

Customers could purchase iconic products that is always available online.

The website definitely allow customers to purchase new collections of products.
