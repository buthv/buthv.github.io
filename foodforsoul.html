Food for Soul

The project is to analyse, define and develop a project strategy for the selected brand. In this case the brand to work on is Food for Soul. The strategy will show how the brand could leverage digital media, design and communication to achieve brand goals and engage target users.

The output of this project requires research, develop and present a highly focused digital proposal. The project must include the following list.
Research & benchmarking
Strategy for digital communication
Technical strategy for development
UX & UI proposal
Working prototype : Mid/High fidelity prototype using Html, CSS, Js, etc.

My role: UX and UI designer , Prototype builder

About Food for Soul

Food for Soul is a non-profit organistion, cultural project in collaboration with communities to fight food waste founded by chef Massimo Bottura and Lara Gilmore. The organisation promotes social awareness about food wastage and hunger through a wide range of initiatives in collaboration with chefs, artisans, food suppliers, artists, designers and institutions. Food for Soul is not a charity project but rather a cultural one.

Our Project Strategy

Our strategy for this project is to targets top change makers including starchitects, top chefs, and designers interested in urban renewal, social inclusion and sustainability.
