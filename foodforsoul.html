Food for soul is the brand selected to analyse, define and develop a project strategy for the brand. The strategy will show how the brand could leverage digital media, design and communication to achieve brand goals and engage target users. The output of this project requires research, develop and present a highly focused digital proposal.
The project must include:
Research & benchmarking
Strategy for digital communication
Technical strategy for development
UX & UI proposal
Working prototype : Mid/High fidelity prototype using Html, CSS, Js, etc.
